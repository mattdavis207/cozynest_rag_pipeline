
CozyNest Home Internal FAQ

Q: Do we offer gift wrapping?
A: Yes, gift wrapping is available during checkout for an additional fee.

Q: Can customers modify orders after purchase?
A: Orders may be modified within 2 hours of placement.

Q: What carriers do we use?
A: UPS, FedEx, and USPS.

Q: Do we offer bundle discounts?
A: Seasonal bundle discounts are periodically available.

Q: Can customers cancel pre-orders?
A: Pre-orders can be canceled before shipment processing begins.
