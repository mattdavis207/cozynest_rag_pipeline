
CozyNest Home Care Instructions

Bedsheets:
- Machine wash cold with like colors.
- Tumble dry low.
- Avoid bleach.

Velvet Curtains:
- Spot clean preferred.
- Steam wrinkles gently.
- Do not machine dry.

Weighted Blankets:
- Wash removable cover separately.
- Air dry recommended.

Pillows:
- Memory foam pillows should not be machine washed.
- Use pillow protectors to extend lifespan.

Sherpa Blankets:
- Wash on delicate cycle.
- Avoid high heat drying to maintain softness.
